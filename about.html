ABOUT ME

Greetings! I am a passionate final semester student majoring in Cadastral and Geodesy Engineering, with a journey characterized by a focus on excellence and innovation. Leadership in the GEIPER research group has been the highlight of my academic experience, providing me with the opportunity to connect with extraordinary individuals and discover my ability to tackle challenges, drive creativity, and foster research.

Since realizing the significance of programming in my career, my interest has evolved into an exciting journey. Starting with C++, exploring Python, and delving into web development during the COVID-19 pandemic, this experience has not only enriched my perspective but also enhanced my skills and knowledge in the fascinating realms of programming and web development.

The practical application of my skills in the Cundinamarca cadastral update process in 2022 unveiled the strategic importance of databases in addressing territorial issues. This discovery has fueled my determination to apply my programming and database knowledge in the context of geomatics and Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

My skills in web development have found tangible applications in academic projects, from presenting final projects to creating and developing this portfolio, as well as in the ongoing construction of the GEIPER group's website, where I play a key role as a leader.

Looking ahead, my focus is on expanding my professional connections and learning from diverse individuals. My goal is to apply my programming and database skills to address specific challenges in the field of geomatics and GIS. My pursuit of excellence drives every action, and I understand that this excellence is an ongoing journey of learning and effective action, rather than the unattainable pursuit of perfection.

In my professional journey, I seek to blend technical expertise with a passion for innovation, contributing to the advancement of geomatics and GIS. Welcome to explore a profile that merges technical skills with a steadfast commitment to growth and excellence.
